Are you tired of witnessing horrendous Pokemon training techniques? Do you cringe at the sight of neglected, mistreated, or poorly trained Pokemon? Then you've come to the right place! PokeShame is dedicated to exposing those trainers who bring shame upon the noble art of Pokemon training.
